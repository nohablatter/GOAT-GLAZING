Savannah James

Savannah James, born in 1986, is the wife of NBA superstar LeBron James and the mother of their three children: Bronny, Bryce, and Zhuri. She is a successful businesswoman, philanthropist, and interior designer. Savannah is known for her elegance, strength, and unwavering support of her husband’s career. Beyond her role as a mother and wife, she is deeply involved in community work, especially empowering young women through education and mentorship programs. Savannah co-founded “Women of Our Future” and has been praised for balancing family life and professional pursuits with grace and confidence. She remains a key pillar in LeBron’s life.
