Bronny James

Bronny James, born in 2004, is the eldest son of LeBron James. A talented basketball player, he played for Sierra Canyon High School and committed to USC. Known for his strong basketball IQ and athleticism, Bronny is pursuing a path toward the NBA, following in his father's footsteps.

Brice Maximus James

Bryce James, born in 2007, is LeBron’s second son. Taller than Bronny, he’s developing into a promising basketball player with a smooth shooting stroke. Bryce plays for high-level AAU teams and shows growing potential, drawing attention as a possible future star in his own right.

Zhuri Nova James

Zhuri James, born in 2014, is LeBron's youngest child and only daughter. Known for her charm and confidence, she has her own YouTube channel called “All Things Zhuri.” Zhuri is often seen in family videos and has become a mini-celebrity with a vibrant personality and creative spirit.
